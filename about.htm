About :-
This website is made to sell and buy books, notes, tutorials. It is a website where students who wants to sell their old books, notes, tutorials can upload the name of the books, notes, tutorials which they want to sell at their desired price. Buyer students can get the informations of their desired book, notes, tutorials and the contact no of seller from whom he or she can buy his/her books. Beside this this website has an extra opportunity to read and download EBOOKS.
